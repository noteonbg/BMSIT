PharmaX - Medication Information

Approved for treating hypertension and heart failure

What is PharmaX?

PharmaX is a prescription medication used primarily to treat high blood pressure (hypertension) and heart failure. It works by relaxing blood vessels, which helps to lower blood pressure and improve the heartâ€™s ability to pump blood.

Usage and Dosage

PharmaX is typically taken once a day with or without food. Your healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dosage for you based on your condition and medical history. Common dosages include:

  • 5 mg per day for mild hypertension
  • 10 mg per day for more severe cases
  • Maximum dosage: 20 mg per day

Side Effects

Like all medications, PharmaX can cause side effects. Common side effects include:

  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Fatigue or tiredness
  • Headache
  • Dry cough (rare)

If you experience any of these side effects or any other unexpected symptoms, please contact your healthcare provider immediately.

Precautions

Before starting PharmaX, inform your doctor if you have any of the following conditions:

  • Kidney disease
  • Liver disease
  • Allergic reactions to similar medications
